Workplace Safety Standards Update 2025

January 15, 2025 Office of Occupational Safety and Health

The Department of Labor announces updated workplace safety standards effective for all employers with more than 50 employees. These revisions incorporate lessons learned from recent enforcement actions and reflect current best practices in occupational health.

Key Changes

The following modifications apply to OSHA Standard 29 CFR 1910:

  • Updated permissible exposure limits for airborne contaminants
  • New requirements for personal protective equipment in construction zones
  • Revised reporting deadlines for workplace incidents
  • Enhanced training requirements for hazardous material handlers

Compliance Timeline

Employers must comply with these updated standards by July 1, 2025. The Department will provide technical assistance and guidance materials during the transition period.
